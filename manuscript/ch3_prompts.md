## 配图 1：流程图：人与AI之间的翻译器（引言部分）
**用途**：说明流程图是将人类模糊意图转化为 AI 可执行动作的中间桥梁。

> **Prompt:**
> A minimalist sketchnote infographic illustration, horizontal 16:9 aspect ratio, 4k high definition. Layout must be clean with plenty of negative space cream beige paper texture background (#FEFCE8). Professional study notes or presentation slide style. Dark brown imperfect hand-drawn outlines, light watercolor textures used for card backgrounds. 
> 
> Content: Three vertical rectangular cards arranged side-by-side horizontally, connected by arrows left to right.
> - Left card (Light pink background): A human stick figure with a messy, tangled yarn ball thought bubble (representing fuzzy thoughts).
> - Middle card (Light yellow background): A glowing blueprint or flowchart acting as a filter/translator.
> - Right card (Light blue background): A cute robot holding a perfectly cut puzzle piece or gear (representing precise execution).
> 
> Text requirements: The image MUST strictly render the following text in legible, bold Simplified Chinese handwriting style. Above the diagram: "流程图 = 人机翻译器". 
> - In Left card write: Title "人类", Subtitle "模糊的意图". Bullet 1: "想法未经历拆解". Bullet 2: "指令空洞泛泛". 
> - In Middle card write: Title "流程图", Subtitle "清晰的施工蓝图". Bullet 1: "明确动作与输入输出". Bullet 2: "消除理解歧义". 
> - In Right card write: Title "AI 员工", Subtitle "精准的执行结果". Bullet 1: "按步骤稳定运转". Bullet 2: "交付超预期成果". 
> No other English text.
